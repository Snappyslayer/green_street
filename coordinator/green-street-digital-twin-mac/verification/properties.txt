Green Street verification properties

1. Safety property
Meaning: the coordinator never commands an undefined brightness level.
LTL: [] (brightness != UNDEFINED)

2. Liveness property
Meaning: if a node becomes online/reachable again, the coordinator eventually resumes adaptive control.
LTL: [] (node_online -> <> adaptive_control)

spin -a verification/coordinator_model.pml
gcc -o pan pan.c
./pan -a
